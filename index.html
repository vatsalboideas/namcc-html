“Our vision has been to present the best of India and the world to our audience.

Completing two incredible years of NMACC as the home of the arts, artists, and the audience, it is an opportune moment to bring the Cultural Weekend to the vibrant city of New York. I warmly welcome you to join us in creating memories with this immersive ode to India's rich cultural legacy.”

- Nita M Ambani

Founder & Chairperson

NMACC Weekend in NYC

The celebrations will commence on September 12th, at the David H. Koch Theater, Lincoln Center, with the highly anticipated U.S. premiere of India’s largest  theatrical production ‘The Great Indian Musical: Civilization to Nation’.
